What is LOOP?

LOOP is a student-built platform with the intent to encourage more people to get involved in outer space research. Our primary focus is curiosity towards the possibility of life on other planets and studying their environments. On this website you can find mini research missions to complete, experiment ebooks, productive space chat, and resources to help you learn more about our universe.

Why It Matters

LOOP helps students use simulations and research to learn more about space, and strengthen scientific thinking skills. People of all ages should have access to a guided STEM platform, so we have experiments ranging from beginner to advanced levels in our ebooks.

LOOP Checklist

Exoplanet Investigation

Pick any exoplanet and run an investigation to conclude whether you think it could support life or not. Share your conclusion with the LOOP community.

Habitat Design

Design a habitat that could support an astronaut living on Mars for a full year.

Invention Creation

Create a simple space-related invention and share it with the LOOP community.

Star Observation

Go outside and pick a star to observe for 10 minutes. Record any changes you notice in brightness or color. Explain what you saw as atmospheric effects, normal star changes, or something unknown.

Planetarium Visit

Visit a planetarium and share something new that you learned about space.

Mini Rocket Engineering

Create a paper or straw rocket and test how different designs affect flight and stability.
